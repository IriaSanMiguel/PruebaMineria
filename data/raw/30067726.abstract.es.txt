The purpose of this study was to determine the effect of the implant material BioOsteo<sup>®</sup> in combinations with epidermal growth factor (EGF) and ascorbic acid (AA) on the consolidation of a non-critical size bone defect.
A unicortical non-critical bone defect was practiced in the right tibia of Wistar rats and 3 weeks later, a biomechanical property analysis was performed through a three-point bending test.
We found that a 1 time single-dose local application of AA + EGF + BioOsteo, directly over the non-critical bone defect microenvironment improves its repair.
